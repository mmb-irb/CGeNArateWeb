Cookies policy

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of information, with a text file format, sent from a website and stored in the user’s browser, in such a way that the website can consult previous activity of the user (Wikipedia).

More about cookies:

Technical document explaining cookies.
http://www.aboutcookies.org

Blocking and deleting cookies

In general, browsers offer several configuration options regarding cookies. These options allow the user to adjust various levels of privacy, so that, for example, the cookies sent from a site like this one are rejected or cookies that may have been saved are deleted. To change these parameters, you can consult the help tool on your browser or request information from its developers.

However, from here you can get the instructions for the most popular desktop and cell phone browsers.

Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer and Safari.

IOS, Android and Windows Phone.

If the user restricts or blocks some or all of the cookies of the present website, some of the tools or services provided by the page may not be available.

Cookies we use on this website

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and provide analytics on site usage. The cookies we use are associated exclusively with anonymous data and do not provide access to any personal information.

Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid, _ga_<container-id>)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website — like which pages are viewed and how much time users spend on them. This information helps us improve our site's content and functionality. All data collected is anonymous and used for statistical purposes only.

If you prefer to disable Google Analytics tracking, you can block these cookies in your browser settings or use Google's official opt-out add-on.
